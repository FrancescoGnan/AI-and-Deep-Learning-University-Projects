To debug the program:

Step 1: Add -g flag in compiler/makefile
Step 2: lldb {executable} - (i.e. lldb read_dataset_labels_exec)
Step 3: r {parameteres} - (i.e. r r mnist-datasets/train-labels.idx1-ubyte IO_output/label_out.txt 0)
Step 4: exit or q - exit/quit

Extras: 
    bt - backtrace
    p {variable} - outputs the contents of the variable
